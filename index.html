Basics

Background

Who We Are

We are a small coffee company based out of City, State that specializes in sourcing high quality coffee from around the world. Ever since our inception, we have made it a point to bring you coffee that you can taste the quality and care put into ever stage of production. All the while ensuring that everyone involved in this process is compensated fairly.

What We Do

We roast coffee ethically sourced from farms all over the world. We emphasize sourcing high quality beans from farms that care as much as we do about the product. We want everyone involved, from the farmer, to you, to be able to have the best experience possible with our products.

Why We Do It

We believe that coffee is best enjoyed when you know it has been grown and processed by people who care, and that you know the money you are spending is going directly to better the lives and communities of those farms.
